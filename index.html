About me

I am a statistician and actuary, with a long-standing interest in using statistics in general insurance and more recently, I’ve used statistics and machine learning techniques in wider areas such as government and social sector analytics.

My original background was in statistical molecular genetics where I used statistical models to detect recombination in bacteria and viruses.
